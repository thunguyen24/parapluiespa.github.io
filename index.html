The Biologique Recherché Methodology - Best solution spa for summer


Skin in summer: bright, exposed, often at risk. Why? Skin is vulnerable to UV radiation year-round, but the summer sun’s increased strength, combined with the increased attractiveness of outdoor pursuits, leaves skin particularly susceptible to damage. Protection of both body and face is paramount. And remember, reapply regularly. The Biologique Recherché Methodology is based on nearly forty years of experience using innovative products and meticulous protocols. The products are formulated with high concentrations of active ingredients derived from natural or biotechnological compounds and are fragrance-free. Skin care is provided following a personal dermocosmetic diagnosis using a Skin Instant© analysis for each individual.
