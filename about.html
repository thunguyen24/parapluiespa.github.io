Finding beauty in the mundane




When we founded Parapluie Spa in 2019, our family of women were at the heart and soul of the brand, beginning with our grandmother Eva who was not only a pioneer in professional-grade skincare, but a dominant force in our lives. When our grandmother retired, she passed on the business and work ethic to our Aunt Lori and Greg’s mom Lisa, who are our mentors. Today, we continue our family’s legacy, which shapes our standard for innovation.

We believe in youth. The optimism in it and the courage to act to protect our future. We believe in being Parapluie. Parapluie is the acknowledgment of our company’s responsibility to the planet and the people. We will make an impact for progress. Inclusivity. Equality. Sustainability. And social justice. Parapluie aims to create a future where our one planet and all our communities coexist, in a just world.

Not just spa space, we take action with Parapluie. Three funds. One core issue each. Infinite solutions to the challenges facing our one planet.

This is our one planet and it’s in crisis. Our earth is warming, biodiversity is being destroyed, and girls and BIPOC communities are bearing the brunt of worsening droughts, floods, fires, and storms. To The Planet Fund donates to philanthropic organizations with science-driven initiatives that make girls, women, and BIPOC communities central to their climate change solutions.
